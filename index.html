Intro

Titillium Web was created at the Academia di Belle Arti di Urbino as a project Course Type design of the Master of Visual Design Campi Visivi. The Academia di Belle Arti di Urbino was established in 1967. It is one of the first founded art academies in Italy. This academy aims to promote painting, sculpture, and scenography. In 1975 the decorations course was established, completing all the study paths. Every year, a dozen or so students work on this font project and fix issues and upkeep it to keep it relevant. Currently 521 million api calls to this font and Titillium Web is used in more than 738,000 websites. The top countires that utilize this font are the United States, Italy, Turkey, Spain, and Germany.
